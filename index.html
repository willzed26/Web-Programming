Lets Start Your Class

NetS is a web based platform intended for educational purposes. Here we provide several features to assist in teaching and learning activities. This platform can be used for all levels of education and educators. NetS also presents an attractive, responsive, and easy to use interface.

Feature


We provide several features that can support the needs of teaching and learning activities.
Here are the main features we offer.


Easy To Use

NetS prioritizes ease of use so you can easily get used to it. NetS also prioritizes user convenience, that's why we made NetS with a simple concept but with complex functionality.

Calendar Reminder

Based on the survey, we found that the problems that often occur to most students are related to time management problems in learning activities. So we made this calendar feature as a reminder for your schedules and assignments.

Making Online Meeting

Make teaching and learning activities feel more real and comfortable by communicating face-to-face in online meetings. Create a virtual meeting room that can accommodate up to 100 attendees.
